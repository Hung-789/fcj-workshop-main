# Event Report: “Saturday Meet up”

### Objectives of the Event

- Comprehensively update the latest technological trends in Cloud Computing and Generative AI globally and within the Vietnamese market.
- Detail standard, modern, and optimized enterprise architecture models on the AWS cloud computing platform.
- Define a clear career roadmap and prepare core skills for students in the Cloud and AI domains.
- Create an interactive space for in-depth networking among technology students, industry experts, and enterprises.

### Event Information

- **Event Name:** Saturday Meet up
- **Date:** Saturday, June 6, 2026
- **Location:** Bitexco Financial Tower, Ho Chi Minh City

### Key Content Highlights

#### Cloud Computing in Enterprises

- Deeply analyze the strong digital transformation trends of enterprises driven by cloud computing infrastructure.
- Clarify the core role of AWS in establishing flexible, secure technology infrastructure with limitless scalability.
- Explore popular and representative cloud services trusted by major corporations in practical operations.

#### Generative AI

- Provide a comprehensive overview of the explosion of Generative AI and its breakthrough applications across multiple fields.
- Explore the potential of combining artificial intelligence with cloud infrastructure to build highly automated and intelligent systems.
- Evaluate the strategic role of AI in supporting software development, big data analysis, and business process optimization.

#### Enterprise Cloud Architectures

- Study benchmark enterprise-scale system architectures built on the AWS platform.
- Examine system design solutions ensuring absolute High Availability and flexible Scalability.
- Master the golden rules of multi-layer security and Cost Optimization within cloud environments.

#### Career Trends

- Clearly define key upcoming job positions:
  - Cloud Engineer
  - Cloud Architect
  - Solutions Architect
  - DevOps Engineer
  - Data Engineer
  - AI Engineer
- Summarize the core technical skills required to succeed in the cloud industry:
  - AWS Cloud Services
  - Linux System Administration
  - Networking & Security
  - Infrastructure as Code (IaC)
  - Automation Tools
  - DevOps Methodologies
  - AI Integration

#### Fireside Chat

- Openly and practically discuss the recruitment demand for high-quality human resources within the current cloud and AI ecosystems.
- Listen to valuable real-world experience-sharing from veteran industry experts.
- Directly ask questions and receive detailed answers regarding career orientation and personal development roadmaps for tech students.

### What Was Learned

#### Professional Knowledge

- Gain an exhaustive understanding of the structure and operational methods of advanced AWS architectural models.
- Deeply recognize the significance and leading role of Cloud Computing in enterprise digital transformation processes.
- Understand practical application methods of Generative AI to solve complex business challenges.
- Realize the close and supportive relationship between cloud technology, AI, and modern IT systems.

#### Career Orientation

- Accurately grasp current labor market conditions and strict recruitment standards within the cloud computing sector.
- Clearly identify the necessary skill sets to confidently pursue a professional career path as a Cloud Engineer or Solutions Architect.
- Strongly reinforce self-study discipline, gaining significant motivation to conquer prestigious AWS certifications alongside hands-on practical skills.

#### Soft Skills

- Practice professional communication skills, questioning methods, and direct exchanges with major tech experts.
- Enhance teamwork mindset, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities within large-scale seminar environments.
- Significantly expand professional networking with like-minded students and representatives from tech enterprises.

### Practical Application to Work

After experiencing and absorbing knowledge from the program, I realized many elements can be directly applied to my personal AWS internship project:

- Flexibly apply distributed and secure cloud architecture principles to the **Network Security Monitoring** system.
- Improve system design perspectives toward scalability and robust data protection on the AWS platform.
- Further research the operational mechanisms of Generative AI to seamlessly integrate intelligent capabilities into **Amazon Bedrock Agent** tasks.
- Continue optimizing core services including AWS Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, and CloudWatch to comprehensively complete the network monitoring system.

### Event Experience

Participating in the **Saturday Meet up** program was truly a meaningful and memorable milestone for me. The event not only helped me stay updated with the latest global technology trends but also provided a sharp practical perspective on how major enterprises deploy and operate systems on AWS.

Through heartfelt sharing sessions from expert speakers, I gained a deeper understanding of modern cloud architectures, the practical value of Generative AI, and the comprehensive landscape of career opportunities in cloud computing. In particular, the open discussion (Fireside Chat) provided me with countless valuable real-world lessons regarding study paths, skill-building, and long-term career strategy planning.

Furthermore, the event's atmosphere opened up an exceptional environment for me to directly interact and learn alongside passionate students and talented engineers currently working directly in the cloud sector, helping me broaden my vision and accumulate valuable practical experience.

### Key Takeaways

- Cloud Computing has affirmed its status as an irreplaceable, core foundation in the digital transformation strategies of all organizations.
- Generative AI will continue acting as a primary driver pushing the development of intelligent, highly automated systems in the near future.
- Pursuing a career in the cloud industry demands a tight, harmonious combination of solid theoretical knowledge and practical project execution experience.
- Actively attending technology workshops and seminars is the optimal method to update new trends, expand perspectives, and capture valuable career opportunities.
